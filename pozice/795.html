Project Procurement Manager

Celá ČR / NěmeckoLogistika a nákupManagement

Your tasks

  • You will prepare technical requirements and specifications for deliveries and services from sub-suppliers
  • You will procure products, deliveries, and services, and generate purchase orders
  • You will issue requests for quotations, conduct price comparisons, and analyze supplier and order data
  • You will negotiate prices and framework agreements with suppliers and subcontractors
  • You will manage and monitor delivery schedules in coordination with project managers
  • You will negotiate adjustments (offers and orders) to ensure both technical and commercial requirements
  • You will prepare bills of quantities and variation offers (variation and claim management)

Requirements

  • Completed secondary or university education, preferably in a technical field
  • Experience in project procurement
  • Knowledge of English and German
  • Technical background
  • Proven experience in negotiation, contract management, claim management, and billing processes
  • Strong communication and commercial skills

We offer

  • Stable employment in a successful international company
  • Opportunity to work on interesting projects
  • Flexible working hours and the option to work from home
  • Opportunities for further education and professional training
  • Attractive salary package
  • 30 days of vacation per year
  • Company pension scheme and many other benefits

Field Service & Site Manager

NěmeckoServis a údržbaManagement

Your tasks

  • Coordination, planning and management of the installation and commissioning
  • Support of projects from implementation to final acceptance, as well as the organization and management of assembly teams on site
  • Monitoring of the professional, timely and quality execution of the work in accordance with planning documents, standards and regulations
  • Carrying out electrical protection and functional tests
  • Fault diagnosis and development of effective solutions to resolve faults
  • Preparation of construction plans, billing of construction services and implementation of acceptances
  • Coordination and monitoring of suppliers on the construction site
  • Regular coordination with customers, service providers and authorities

Requirements

  • Successful completion of education preferably in the electrical or energy sector
  • Professional experience in electrical installations
  • Experience in professional and timely coordination of construction with various contractors
  • Good knowledge of relevant VOB, HOAI, VDE standards and technical regulations
  • Authorized to switch systems up to 30 kV
  • Ability to communicate in English or German
  • Confident demeanor, strong communication skills, commitment and flexibility

We offer

  • Stable employment in a successful international company
  • Opportunity to work on interesting projects
  • Flexible working hours and the option to work from home
  • Opportunities for further education and training
  • Provision of mobile devices (laptop, mobile phone)
  • Company car
  • 30 days holiday per year
  • Attractive company pension scheme and many other benefits

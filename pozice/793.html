Project Manager

NěmeckoProjekty a stavebnictvíManagement

Your tasks

  • Overall responsibility for technical, scheduling and economic implementation from planning to commissioning on site
  • Technical coordination with design, engineering and commissioning
  • Awarding of subcontractor services to subcontractors
  • Supervision of customer acceptance tests and on site
  • Quality management and assessment, tracking and documentation of project risks as well as implementation of risk countering measures
  • Scheduling, cost estimation, project controlling and reporting to division management
  • Customer, claim and change management

Requirements

  • Completed education in a top electrical engineering field
  • Experience in a similar position in project management
  • Experience in leading project teams, contract management, handling claims, and invoicing procedures is an advantage
  • Experience in the professional and timely coordination of project phases such as bid preparation, project planning, procurement, implementation, and commissioning
  • Ability to communicate in English or German
  • Strong persuasive and assertive abilities
  • Excellent communication and business skills

We offer

  • Stable employment in a successful international company
  • Opportunity to work on interesting projects
  • Flexible working hours and the option to work from home
  • Opportunities for further education and professional training both domestically and abroad
  • Attractive salary package
  • 30 days of vacation per year
  • Company pension scheme and many other benefits
